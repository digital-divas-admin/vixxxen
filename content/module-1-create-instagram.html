Step-by-Step Setup

1 Download Instagram

Download the Instagram app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). You can also sign up at instagram.com on desktop.

2 Create Your Account

Tap "Create new account" and choose to sign up with:

  • Email (recommended) - Use a dedicated email for your AI brand
  • Phone number - Quick setup but harder to manage multiple accounts
  • Facebook - Links to your existing Facebook account
3 Choose Your Username

Your username (@handle) is how people find and tag you. Choose wisely:

  • ‚úì Keep it short and memorable
  • ‚úì Make it relevant to your niche or character name
  • ‚úì Avoid excessive numbers or underscores
  • ‚úì Check if it's available on other platforms too
üí° Tip: If your ideal username is taken, try adding "official", "ai", "studio", or your niche (e.g., @jessicafitai, @aiwithjessica).
4 Switch to Business Account

Once your account is created, switch to Business:

  • Go to your Profile ‚Üí tap the ‚ò∞ menu (top right)
  • Tap Settings and privacy
  • Tap Account type and tools
  • Tap Switch to professional account
  • Select Business (not Creator)
  • Choose a category that fits (e.g., "Digital Creator", "Art", "Entertainment")
  • Add contact info or skip for now
5 Set Up Your Profile

Complete your profile basics:

  • Profile Photo: Use an AI-generated avatar or logo that represents your brand
  • Display Name: Your character or brand name (can include emojis)
  • Bio: 150 characters to describe what you do. Include a call-to-action
  • Link: Add your website, Linktree, or other social links
üí° Bio Formula: [What you create] + [Who it's for] + [Call to action]

6 Adjust Privacy Settings

As a Business account, your profile is public by default. Review these settings:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy
  • Review Comments - filter offensive comments and keywords
  • Review Messages - decide who can DM you
  • Review Tags and Mentions - control who can tag you
